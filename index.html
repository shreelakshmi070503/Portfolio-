Hospital Management UI

The Hospital Management UI was designed to help hospitals handle their daily operations in a faster and more organized way. The system supports important tasks like booking patient appointments, checking doctor availability, managing medical records, and handling emergency cases. The main goal was to create a clean and easy-to-use interface that helps hospital staff work without confusion or delays. The system has two main user roles: Doctor Side and Patient Side. On the Doctor Side, doctors can view their appointment schedules, check patient details, update treatment information, and manage test reports. They also receive alerts for emergency cases so they can respond quickly. On the Patient Side, users can book appointments, see their upcoming visits, view doctor profiles, and access their medical records and test results in a simple and secure way. While designing the interface, I focused on making the layout clear and readable by using proper spacing, clean typography, and helpful icons. The dashboard was created to give each user a quick overview of all important information on a single screen. I tested the design to make sure users could easily find important features like emergency help and appointment requests.
